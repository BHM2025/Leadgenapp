🔌 Setup & Connections

Connect these services once — then everything runs automatically. Estimated setup time: 2–3 hours.

📋 How This All Works Together

GoHighLevel is the automation backbone — it captures leads from your landing pages, runs email/SMS sequences, and holds your CRM pipeline. Facebook/Google ad leads flow directly into GHL via native integrations. Claude AI scores each lead via webhook and writes your campaigns. This dashboard reads your GHL data via API so you see everything in one place. You only touch the system when a lead is HOT and ready to sell.

3️⃣ Facebook Lead Ads → GoHighLevel (Native Integration)

No API coding needed

GHL has a built-in Facebook integration. You connect Facebook once inside GHL, and every Facebook Lead Ad submission is automatically sent to GHL as a new contact — triggering your automation workflows.

1

Inside GHL: Settings → Integrations → Facebook

Click Connect, log in with your Facebook Business account, and select your ad account. Done — all lead forms are now synced.

2

In Facebook Ads Manager: Create Lead Ad Campaign

Objective: Lead Generation. Create your lead form with fields: Name, Phone, Email, Monthly Bill, Property Owner (Yes/No). The exact copy is in the Campaigns section of this platform.

3

Map the fields in GHL

GHL will show you a field mapping screen. Map Facebook's "Full Name" → GHL Contact Name, "Phone Number" → Phone, etc. This is a dropdown UI — no coding.

4

That's it — leads flow in automatically

Every Facebook lead submission now creates a GHL contact, triggers the AI scoring webhook, and starts the email/SMS drip. Zero manual work.

4️⃣ Google Lead Form Ads → GoHighLevel
1

GHL: Settings → Integrations → Google

Connect your Google Ads account. GHL syncs Google Lead Form Extensions automatically — same process as Facebook.

2

In Google Ads: Create Search Campaign with Lead Form Extension

Add a Lead Form Extension to your search campaign. Fields: Name, Phone, Email. Target keywords like "solar panels near me", "solar installation near me". Copy is in the Campaigns section.
